How do armed professionals maintain the trust of the American people?

By living and holding the army ethic

What is the Army ethic?

The Army ethic is an evolving set of laws, values, and beliefs deeply embedded into the core of the Army culture and practiced by all members of the armed profession to motivate and guide the appropriate conduct of individual members bound together by a common moral purpose.
