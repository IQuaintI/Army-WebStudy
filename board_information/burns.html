You are evaluating a casualty with burns. There is clothing stuck to the wound. What should you do?

Leave the clothing in place, do not attempt to remove

What are the four types of burns?

1. Thermal
2. Electrical
3. Chemical
4. Laser

If a casualty is the victim of an electrical burn, what should you do first?

Ensure that the power source causing the burn is turned off

What should be done for liquid chemical burns?

Remove the liquid by flushing with water
