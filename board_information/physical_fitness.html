Physical Training - APFT(AR 350-1, FM 7-22, ATP 7-22.01, ATP 7-22.02)

Home

In what order must an APFT be performed?

1. Push-ups
2. Sit-ups
3. 2-mile run

What are the commands for the extended rectangular formation?

1. Extend to the left, March
2. Arms downward, move
3. Left face
4. Extend to the left, March
5. Arms downward, move
6. Right face
7. Count off
8. Even numbers to the left uncovered

What is the command to re-assemble the extended rectangular formation?

Assemble to the right, march

What is the minimal number of Soldiers required to conduct an APFT?

4

Name four times when a Soldier should not be tested for the APFT?

1. When ill or fatigued
2. When they have participated in tiring duties
3. When weather or envoronmental conditions are hazardous
4. When it would violate medical profile

How often must a Soldier take the APFT at a minimum?

At least once every six months

Before taking an APFT, what must the commander specify?

If the test is for record

What does the acronym PPPT stand for?

The Pregnancy Postpartum Physical Training Program

What action is taken if a Soldier cannot participate in a physical fitness training because of a profile?

The Soldier will be placed in a rehabilitative and reconditioning program within the profile guidelines given by the healthcare commander and be supervised by the unit commander

The 2-mile run course should be free of what type of hazards?

1. Traffic
2. Slippery surfaces
3. Heavy pollution

What manual does AR 350-1 direct leaders to use when conducting Army physical fitness training?

FM 7-22

What constitutes a repetative APFT failure?

When a Soldier has been provided adequate time and assistance (not to exceed 90 days) and fails a retest

If a Soldier is in a military school and fails to meet APFT or weight control standards, they will be dismissed and the 1059 will be annotated as failed to achieve course standards. How long must they wait to reattend the course if they are a first-time failure? How long for the second failure?

First time failure must wait 6 months while a second failure must wait 12 months

WHat action is taken who fails a record APFT or fail to take a record within 12 months?

The Soldier will be flagged in accordance with AR 600-8-2

What are the requirements for earning the ARMY physical fitness badge?

Soldiers must score 270 or above with a minimum of 90 points per event on the APFT

What action is taken if a Soldier fails a record APFT and then fails a retest?

The Soldier will be barred from re-enlistment or processed for separation from service

How often may a commander administer the APFT?

As often as they wish, but they must specify beforehand when the APFT is for record

How many stopwatches are required for an APFT?

Two

With regards to the Army Body Composition Program (ABCP) what should commanders avoid doing?

Placing all special needs Soldiers in the same conditioning program - more exercise will not automatically result in improved body composition

How will a Soldier be scored if they become ill during the APFT and cannot complete the test?

They are a test failure

If a Soldier is profiled for two or more APFT events what does their APFT consists of?

Two mile run or an alternative aerobic event

What are the requirements for the APFT running the course?

A flat, measured two mile running course with a solid surface with no more than a 3% grade

When may a mat be used during the APFT?

During the push-up and sit-up events so long as the entire body is on the mat
