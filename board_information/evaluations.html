Evaluations (AR 600-20, AR 623-3, DA PAM 623-3)

Home

When can a relief for cause action be taken?

When a commander loses confidence in a subordinate's ability to command due to misconduct, poor judgement, inability to complete assigned duties, or similar reasons

In most cases, what is required prior to coniducting a relief for cuase?

Counseling unless it is deemed inapproriate or not practical under the circumstances

What regulation covers the evaluation reporting system?

AR 623-3

How will the rating chain be structured?

They will correspond with the chain of command or chains of supervision

When can retroactive changes be made to the rating scheme?

Never

What support forms will a Soldier recieve at the beginning of the rated period?

A copy of the rater's and senior rater's suppor form

When will an initial NCOER counseling be conducted?

Initially within 30 days of the start of the rating period and quarterly thereafter

Who will normally be appointed a Soldier's rater?

Their immediate supervisor

How is pooling used in the evaluation system?

Pooling is illegal and is not to be used in the evaluation system

How many days of obvservation must a Senior Rater have to rate Soldier?

A minimum of 60 calender days

What document tells a rated Soldier who their supplementary reviewer is?

The published rating scheme

What is the normal minimum rating period for an evaluation period?

90 days

WHat form is used to document the initial and quarterly counseling sessions for an NCOER?

DA Form 2166-9-1A

What action may the reviewer take when there are discrepancies in the evaluation report that they believe are not consistent with the evaluation process?

They can attach a memorandum as an external attachment for submission to HQDA

What are the two types of evaluation reports?

Mandatory and optional

Name three special interest items that will be included in the counseling for an evaluation process?

1. Safety
2. Individual readiness
3. Energy informed actions
4. Support of behavioral health goals
5. Support of EEO
6. Suppot of equal opportunity
7. Support of SHARP programs

What information will the rated Soldier provide to the rater after the initial counseling session?

The rated Soldier is responsible for drafting performance goals and expectations and providing major performance objectives. They should use the rater and senior rater support forms to help them accomplish this goal

What documents does the rated Soldier use when developing goals and objectives?

The rater and senior rater support forums

How will the rated NCO verify the face-to-face counseling sessions with the rater?

By dating and initialing the support form or the DA Form 2166-9-1A

Who maintains a personal working copy of the NCO support form?

The rated NCO

How often will follow-up counseling be conducted?

Quarterly

An NCO will never complete their own evaluation report however they may supply the rater with what information?

They may supply a list of their achievments and accomplishments for the rated period

How is performance evaluated by the rater?

The rater will consider the results achieved, how they were achieved, and how well the NCO complied with professional standards

Who is responsible for rating the area of potential on the evaluation form?

The senior rater

Who has the overarching role of ensuring compliance with the evaulating reporting process, policy guidelines, of AR 623-3, and the procedural guidance of DA Pam 623-3?

The supplementary reviewer

If a Soldier seeks voluntary mental health counseling for a condition not detected by the chain of command what comments may be entered on the evaluation report?

None.It will not be placed on the evaluation report as this would discourage self-referral to obtain assistance from healthcare professionals

Name three of five incidents that REQUIRE comments on an evaluation report when substantiated by an Army investigation?

Substantiated findings of:
1. EO complaints
2. Findings of sexual harassment and/or assault
3. Failure to report sexual harassment and/or sexual assault
4. Failure to respond to a complaint or report of sexua harassment and/or sexual assault
5. Retaliation against a person making a complaint or report of sexual harassment and/or sexual assault

What chapter of AR 623-3 covers the appeal process?

Chapter 4

What is the purpose of the commander's inquiry?

It provides a greater degree of command involvement and prevents injustices and corrects errors before the report becomes a matter of permanent record

What action must the commander take if they believe an evaluation report might be illegal, unjust, or in violation of the evaluation regulation?

A commander's inquiry must be conducted

Who can require a change be made to an evaluation report?

No one can require changes be made to an evaluation report

Who has access to evaluation reports?

The rating chain, the rated Soldier, and those that process the report

To qualify as a rater, the rater must be assigned for a minimum of how many calender days?

90 calender days

Define additional duties

Additional responsibilities not normally associated with the duty description

What is a commanders inquiry?

An investigation into a Soldier's evaluation report made by officials in the chain of command to determine if something in the report is illegal, unjust, or in violation of regulation

How is a Soldier evaluated when they are on leave for a period of more than 30 days?

This period of time is considered non-rated time

What is a rater tendency report?

A document provided by HQDA for raters of NCOs. It shows the rater's rating assessment history by grade of previous NCOs rated

What is redress?

The process by which a Soldier can address errors, bias, or injustices during and after the preparation of an evaluation report and have them corrected

Define relief

The removal of a Soldier from a position due to loss of confidence based on the Soldiers personal or professional conduct, behavior or performance. Requires a Relief for Cause report

What action must take place if there is no uniformed Army designated official in the rating scheme?

A supplementary review must be conducted
