When can a relief for cause action be taken?

When a commander loses confidence in a subordinate's ability to command due to misconduct, poor judgement, inability to complete assigned duties, or similar reasons

In most cases, what is required prior to coniducting a relief for cuase?

Counseling unless it is deemed inapproriate or not practical under the circumstances

What regulation covers the evaluation reporting system?

AR 623-3

How will the rating chain be structured?

They will correspond with the chain of command or chains of supervision

When can retroactive changes be made to the rating scheme?

Never

What support forms will a Soldier recieve at the beginning of the rated period?

A copy of the rater's and senior rater's suppor form

When will an initial NCOER counseling be conducted?

Initially within 30 days of the start of the rating period and quarterly thereafter

Who will normally be appointed a Soldier's rater?

Their immediate supervisor
