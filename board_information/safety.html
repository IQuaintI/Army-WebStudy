At a minimum, how often will a Soldier's privately owned vehicle be inspected by the unit?

At least 6 months

What item will be checked during a unit inspection of a Soldier's privately owned vehicle?

Verification of a driver's license, insurance, and registration. The unit will also inspect the local vehicle checklists

Explain the impact of the Army Safety Program?

It has more impact on Soldier Readiness than any other program. Every Soldier and Army civilian is responsible to stop unsafe acts by being responsible for accident prevention

Define risk management

The Army process for identifying, assessing, and controlling risks and then making decisions that balance risk cost with benefits
